# About This Project

A deep dive into print design and how traditional media can still make a powerful impact in a digital-first world.

## The Challenge

Create a series of posters that would capture attention and communicate complex ideas quickly and effectively.

## The Solution

We used bold typography, striking color combinations, and minimalist compositions to create a series that works at multiple scales.

## The Result

The posters were displayed at a local design exhibition and received positive feedback from both peers and industry professionals.
